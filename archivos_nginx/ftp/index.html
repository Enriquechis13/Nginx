MMA

The Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) is a premier mixed martial arts (MMA) organization, known globally for its intense combat events. Founded in 1993, the UFC brought MMA into the mainstream, allowing fighters from various martial arts backgrounds (such as boxing, Brazilian jiu-jitsu, wrestling, and muay thai) to compete against each other. Bouts are held within an eight-sided cage called the Octagon, which has become symbolic of the organization. Fighters employ a range of striking, grappling, and submission techniques, making MMA one of the most dynamic and versatile combat sports.

The UFC hosts events worldwide, with major shows called Pay-Per-View (PPV)
events, and others as Fight Nights, featuring fighters and emerging talents.
